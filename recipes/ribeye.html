Ribeye

Nothing says dinner like a thick steak and a side of mashed potatoes

The sous vide is my cooking method of choice as I can always get a perfectly cooked steak extremely easily. Finish it off in a cast-iron and you're done! Easy peasy!

Ingredients

  • Ribeye
  • Vegetable oil
  • Butter
  • Thyme
  • Rosemary
  • Salt and pepper

Equipment

  • Cast-iron pan
  • Sous vide

Steps

  1. Turn sous vide up to 125°F (for medium rare).
  2. Vacuum seal salt and peppered ribeye with thyme and rosemary. Place in water bath for 1-2 hours.
  3. Heat small layer of oil in cast iron pan until around 500°F. Place in ribeye as well as a tbsp of butter.
  4. Sear each side until a nice crust forms. Remove from pan and wrap in aluminum foil for 10 minutes. This will give the steak time to rise to an internal temperature of 135°F.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
